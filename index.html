Why I started Flow IQ

Founder note

Inside the bank, I kept seeing the same pattern: brilliant people doing repetitive, low-leverage work because the tools they relied on weren't designed for the complexity of modern client portfolios. Onboarding, KYC review, renewals, referrals — every workflow leaked time and signal.

I started Flow IQ to close that gap — to bring real AI to the workflows that actually move the business. Not chatbots. Not demos. Working intelligence, built by someone who has to live with the results.

— Ribesh, Founder & AVP
